Banana Shake



A fresh delicious treat

Curtido is a salad made out of cabbage and served with El Salvadoran pupusas. Stuff cooked pupusas with the curtido for a delicious treat!

Ingredients

  • 2 pcs of egg
  • 1 cabbage
  • 2 carrots
  • oyster sauce
  • soy sauce
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 1 cup of water

Directions

  1. Combine the cabbage and carrot in a large bowl and pour the boiling water over the mixture.
  2. Allow the mixture to steep for 5 minutes; drain well.
  3. Return the cabbage and carrots to the bowl.
  4. Mix in the green onion, vinegar, 1/2 cup of water, and oregano.
  5. Toss until all ingredients are combined.
  6. Chill for 20 minutes before serving.
